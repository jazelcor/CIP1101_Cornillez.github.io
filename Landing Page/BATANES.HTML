Batanes




Batanes is an archipelagic province in the Philippines, administratively part of the Cagayan Valley region. It is the northernmost province in the country, and also the smallest, both in population and land area. Its capital is Basco located on the island of Batan. Batanes is famous for its breathtaking views of the wonders of nature such as the vast rolling hills, amazing lighthouses, the amazing view of the Pacific Ocean, and the kidnest locals you'd ever meet.


  • How to get there:
    • Take the train from Doroteo Jose LRT to Edsa Lrt.
    • Fly from Manila (MNL) to Basco (BSO) MNL - BSO.

  • Places to visit in Batanes:
    • Morong Beach
    • Mount Iraya
    • Basco Lighthouse and Sabtang Lighthouse
    • Vayang Rolling Hills
    • Valugan Boulder Beach
    • Mt. Carmel Chapel or Tukon Church
    • Chawa View Deck
    • Savidug Stone Houses
    • Honesty Coffee Shop
    • Songsong Ruins
