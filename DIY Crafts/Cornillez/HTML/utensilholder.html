Kitchen Utensil Holder


Most mothers are creative by nature, doing home crafts to organize and make their place tidy is important for their home. Because the kitchen is the heart of any home where moms spend a lot of time they do all their best to make their kitchen organized and cooking pleasant. The placement of utensils, kitchen tools and supplies of food are a bit tricky, especially for a small space.

The first step to make a big difference in your house is to start with the most important part in it which is the kitchen.

What you need


  • Thick Yarn
  • Hook
  • Empty Can
  • Pair of Scissors


Procedure


  1. Crochet a chain. The length of the chain should fit the
           perimeter of your can.

  2. When you get the right length of the chain, close it to
           make a circle with a slip stitch.

  3. Now just work around with single crochet* until you get
           the right height. *Crochet into the back loop of the
           stitches only.

  4. When you have your “crochet cylinder” ready, weave
           in the ends.

  5. Choose which side you want to be the right side. Then
           just put the “crochet cylinder” on the can, double in the
           edge of the “crochet cylinder” to hide the metal edge
           of the can.

  6. Your kitchen holder is ready!
