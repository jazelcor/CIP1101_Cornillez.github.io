Framed Earring Holder


Tired of digging through your jewelry box for earrings? Why not hang them on the wall in a beautiful frame?

Organize all your earrings in various shapes and sizes by placing them in cool earring organizers. This idea will help you keep your dressing table clutter free and lessen your search for a specific earring that goes with your dress when you do not want to be late for a party or a date.

What you need


  • Any size frame
  • White Glue
  • Painters tape
  • Pair of Scissors
  • Cross stitch fabric


Procedure


  1. Use an old frame to begin with

  2. Remove the stand

  3. Cut a cross stitch fabric with the same size of the frame.

  4. Lock it in.

  5. Hang your favorite earrings.
