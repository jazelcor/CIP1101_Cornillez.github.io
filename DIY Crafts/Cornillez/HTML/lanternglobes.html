Lantern Globes


Want to make a cool hanging light fixture for your house? Or decorative globes for a room? It's easy and inexpensive! Fair warning, it’s a super duper messy project — like you’re hands will be covered in sticky-goo and your work space will be speckled with gluey-drops-of-glueness kind of messy — but the mess and cleanup are well worth the effort. Just invest in a cheap plastic drop cloth and a pair of plastic gloves.

What you need


  • Yarn
  • Glue
  • Bouncing ball
  • Hanging Light Fixture
  • LED Bulb
  • 2 piece clamp connector
  • Washer


Procedure


  1. Make a mark at the top and side of the ball.
  2. Mix both glue and water on a container
  3. Soak the yarn on the container
  4. Start covering the entire ball with a yarn and avoiding the        mark at the top until you make it to the last string.
  5. Let it dry. This can take up to 2 days.

  6. Deflate the ball from the marks on the side and place a
           hook on the top of it.
  7. Slide the washer into the light fixer and take the
           clamp connector and clamp it around so that it will not move.
  8. Place the bulb properly and plug it in.
  9. Hang it in your house.
