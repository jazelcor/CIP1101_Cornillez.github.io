Festive Banners






This banner is easy to make! You only need a few materials
to do it. Banners are made of triangular shape paper with
different colors to match the theme of the festival, hang onto
public place when a festival is happening.




Go Back To Holiday



Materials Needed:


  • Scrapbook Paper
  • Scissors
  • String

Procedure


  • Cut the papers into triangular shape.
  • Make a few holes on the bottom of the triangle.
  • Attach the flag to the strings.
  • Lastly, hang it the banners.
