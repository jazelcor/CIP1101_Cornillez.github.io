Snowman of Cups






A Snow Sculpture is often built-in regions with sufficientsnowfall.
In many places, typical snowmen consist of three large snowballs
of different sizes with some additional accoutrements for facial
and other features. But this time the snowman will be built out of
cups.





Go Back To Holiday





Materials Needed:


  • Scarf
  • Bunch of Cups
  • Stapler
  • Scissors
  • Glue Gun
  • Color Paper

Procedure


  • Draw a circle guide on how big the snowman (optional).
  • Staple each cup to each other until it forms a dome.
  • Do another one but only go halfway, then staple it to the first made dome.
  • Repeat step 2 and 3 but with smaller cups.
  • Combine the two to form the snowman.
  • Cut color paper into circles for the eyes,and body. While carrot shape for the nose.
  • Make a hat and put the scarf on it.
