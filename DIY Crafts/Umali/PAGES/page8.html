Party hats



Birthday party without party hats? That can be boring, but buying party hats can be expensive. This year, why not make your own?. Homemade party hats are easy, fun, creative, and reusable. It is so easy that these craft will only take about a few minutes to acomplish!

Materials that you need:


  • Card stock paper
  • Stapler
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • String
  • Decorative items of your choice


Procedure


  1. Cut the corners off of the card stock to form a large circle. Then cut a narrow triangle starting from the outside to the center.

  2. Fold the paper to form a cone shape by overlapping the two cut cornersâ€”the two bottom edges of the triangle. How far you overlap determines the fit of the hat. Staple when finished.

  3. Add any of your decorations. Ex: glitter, jewels, stickers, string

  4. Cut two circular holes on each side of the bottom of the party hat. Then gather your string (measure length to fit head), and then tie knots on each side to secure.
