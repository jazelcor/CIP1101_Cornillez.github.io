Paper Mache Strawberry Piñata



This strawberry piñata is the perfect size—not too big, not too small, and you can adjust the size based on how big you blow the balloon. Paper mache piñatas are the most traditional (and the messiest), but make for a great pre-party craft activity. It is one of the easiest piñatas to make and a great choice for beginners. This craft is so easy it would only take you a few minutes to do!


Materials that you need:


  • Ballon
  • Glue
  • Flour
  • Newspapers
  • Scissors
  • Twine
  • Water
  • Tissue paper
  • Candy
  • X-acto


Procedure


  1. Dip newspaper into paste. Place newspaper on balloon.

  2. Cover balloon leaving space at the top.

  3. Fold tissue paper.

  4. Cut strips of tissue paper.

  5. Make slits for garland.

  6. Glue garland onto balloon.

  7. Cover the entire balloon except top.

  8. Cut out leaves, seed and stem.

  9. Add seeds to tissue paper.

  10. Pop balloon.

  11. Make holes for hanging twine.

  12. Fill with candy.

  13. Cover top with stem and leaves.
