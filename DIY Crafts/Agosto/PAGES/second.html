Homemade Chalk Paint



This is an easy DIY that can entertain the children all day long this summer. This DIY may improve your children’s creativity, it also makes the sidewalk looks pleasing, and lastly, your children will surely enjoy doing it. Below are the materials and procedures to make a homemade sidewalk chalk paint.

What you need:


  • Muffin Tin
  • 1/2 cup Cornstarch
  • 2/3 cup Water
  • Liquid Food Coloring


Procedure


  1. Mix together the water and cornstarch, and stir until the cornstarch dissolves.

  2. Divide the paint mixture into the wells of a muffin tin or similar painting tray.

  3. Add food coloring to achieve the desired colors – note that the colors will dry much lighter on the sidewalk than they appear in the tin.
