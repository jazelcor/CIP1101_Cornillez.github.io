Spiral Suncatchers



This DIY is a hanging decoration, usually displayed in windows. From the word itself this decoration is used to catch the sunlight and form rainbows or pretty reflections to appear in your room. This DIY comes in all shapes, sizes and styles but almost always use beads in their design. Kids will surely enjoy making this DIY! Below are the materials and procedures to make the spiral suncatchers.

What you need:


  • Memory wire
  • Head pin
  • Large glass or faceted bead
  • Beads
  • Pliers


Procedure


  1. Thread the faceted bead onto the headpin and secure it.

  2. Using the pliers form a loop at one end of the memory wire.

  3. Thread the large bead on and close the loop.

  4. Thread beads onto the memory wire.

  5. Form a loop at the end.

  6. Thread string or wire through the end loop, and hang the sun catcher up.
