Mini Wall Shelf


If you are looking for some truly unique and gorgeous décor for any room, this DIY popsicle stick shelf is perfect. This is a simple project that is so much fun to make – even the kids could help with this one. You can keep this shelf in your office room or living room or a bedroom. Popsicle sticks are great to crafts with. They are cheap and can be used to make just anything. This hexagon wall shelf is a great way of keeping kids books or displaying small home decor item.

What you need


  • Popsicle sticks
  • Glue
  • Paper as base


Procedure


  1. Create a hexagon-shaped popsicle sticks
  2. Put glue on each sides.
  3. Do it again with several hexagon-shaped popsicle sticks
  4. Have it glued and let it dry.
