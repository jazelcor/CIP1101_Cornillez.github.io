Skewers Photo Holder


Picture frames are a great way to show off your favorite pictures, but they can get expensive. Making them rather than buying them is the best way to get a nice frame that perfectly fits your picture without breaking your wallet. Frames make perfect gifts for loved ones, or keep them to adorn your own walls by creating your photo holder.

What you need


  • Paper
  • Skewers
  • Glue gun
  • Pair of Scissors
  • Wooden clips


Procedure


  1. Create a grid like frame on the base paper.
  2. Connect the skewers using a glue gun
  3. Add some color and design.
  4. Cutout pictures of your choice and place it using the clips.
