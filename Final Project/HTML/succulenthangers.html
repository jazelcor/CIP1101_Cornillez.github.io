Faux Succulent Hangers

What you need:

  • Faux succulents
  • Wood boxes
  • Foam brush
  • Craft paint
  • Hot Glue
  • Wire cutters


Procedure

  1. Separate Your Succulents
  2. Use your wire cutter to snip at the wire stems at the bases to remove them from their original design.
  3. Set them aside.
  4. Paint Your Wood Boxes.
  5. Let the boxes dry completely.
  6. Plan Out Your Design.
  7. Glue Your Succulents.
  8. Stick them inside the wood boxes and let cool.
  9. You can use a simple wall hanging strip stuck to the back of each box to hang.
