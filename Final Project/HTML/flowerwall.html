Flower Wall Decor

What you need:

  • Construction papers
  • Scissor
  • Craft Glue
  • A Ribbon


Procedure

  1. Cut some petals out of small Squares.
  2. Fold them in Cone form.
  3. Paste the cones on a circular paper piece.
  4. Keep on pasting the cones and you will get your flower.
  5. Now, Place the flowers on a Paper ring.
