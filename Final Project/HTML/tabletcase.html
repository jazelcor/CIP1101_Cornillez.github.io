Tablet Case

What you need:

  • An old book
  • Hot glue sticks
  • Felt Paper
  • White Glue
  • Elastic
  • Pair of Scissors


Procedure

  1. Slowly remove the pages of the old book.
  2. Line the Inside Cover.
  3. Glue the felt to the inside of the book.
  4. Make sure to include a little extra felt in the spine of the book so that it can close.
  5. Place your e-reader on the inside cover (on the right) and measure out where the corners of the device hit.
  6. You want four elastic pieces to stretch over each of the corners.
  7. Attach the elastic, use the point of your scissors and poke holes where you're like the elastic to go through the cover.
  8. Tie the elastic behind the cover and knot
