Gift Wrapping Decoration






Ribbons usually comes with a gift wrapper when wrapping
present to please theeyes of the receiver, and this one is
called paper bow.





Go Back To Holiday





Materials Needed:


  • Color Paper
  • Scissors
  • Glue

Procedure


  • Fold the paper horizontally 3 times for the guide.
  • Cut the paper based on the guide.
  • Make the half of the pieces smaller than the other half.
  • Glue them on each end to form a circle, then glue again on the center to form infinity sign.
  • Do this to all the pieces.
  • Combine the larger ones with glue on the center like your drawing a star.
  • Add the smaller ones like your painting a star.
  • Do a small circle and put it on top to finish the product.
