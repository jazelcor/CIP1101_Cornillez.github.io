An easy to make Christmas Card Holder out of hanger.


Materials Needed:


  • Hanger
  • Tape
  • Small clothes pins
  • Festive ornament(optional)

Procedure


  • Arrange hangers in the shape of a tree
  • Then attach using tape
  • Use small clothes pins to hang cards from the tree
  • Stick a festive ornament on top.
