Centerpiece




Description - an important item of a display, usually of a table setting.
Centerpieces help set the theme of the decorations and bring extra
decorations to the room. A centerpiece also refers to any central or
important object in a collection of items.


Materials Needed:


  • Large Glass
  • Wine Glass
  • Candle Holder
  • Decorations (silver balls, White Magnolia Bouquet, 7-Stem white Rose Bush, Onion Grass Sprays, Berry Bouquet, Red Blooming Branches, Holiday Silver Branches) .
  • Foam
  • Glue

Procedure


  • Glue the glass piece by pair.
  • After it dry, fill the glass with silver balls.
  • Put the foam inside the glass.
  • Then put your decorations one by one.
