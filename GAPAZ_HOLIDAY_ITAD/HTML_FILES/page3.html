Festive Throw Pillow




Description - a small, decorative soft furnishing item made
from a wide range of textiles including cotton, linen, silk, leather,
microfiber, suede, chenille, and velvet. , designed with a
poinsettia and a bell.


Materials Needed:


  • Pillow
  • Felt
  • Hem Tape
  • Jingle Bells
  • Thread
  • Fabric Glue

Procedure


  • Make three leaf sizes.
  • Trace each of the leaf over the felt.
  • Trim around each leaf.
  • Glue the two sides together.
  • Stitch down each of the center of the leaf.
  • Use hem tape to put the leaf to the pillow.
  • String the bells onto the thread.
  • Hand sew the bells on the center of the flower.
